About Simolola Trading


SimololaTrading is a company formed to provide professional services in the mining and engineering industry. We provide a variety of Personal Protective Equipment and Mining and Engineering consumables. We also specialize in providing services in Vamping, Reclamation, Rail Maintenance, Main tips as well as in stope tip and box front construction and repairs.
